El viaje de Dante (Dante's journey)

Dante is always looking at the new map his grandad gave him or playing with his globe, dreaming about seeing the world. He is so intrigued. How do kids have fun in China? Do they play catch in Lisbon? Is the rain in Tangier as wet as here? Does the night always follow the day in Nigeria? He thinks and wonders through seas of doubt and wonderment

Written by Bárbara Gelbaum and Pablo Stefano.
Published by Catapulta Editores in 2022.
