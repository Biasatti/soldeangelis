Manual de cocina ilustrado (Illustrated cooking journal)

An illustrated guide for cooking beginners. What do I need? What does searing, sautéing, pan-frying even mean? A cook book that takes the time to explain everything you need to know to fall in love with the art of cooking or to survive in a terrain that may not be for you, but you still have to conquer. Cooking for beginners made easy with drawings and charts.

Written by Victoria Rey.
Published by Catapulta Editores in 2020.
